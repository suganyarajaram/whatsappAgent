What Makes This Different From v1

Version 1 showed a customer facing agent. Version 2 shows the full picture. The merchant can now manage their entire restaurant through natural language commands — the same AI intelligence that serves customers also serves the business owner. This is the bidirectional AI platform that Wati is positioned to build but has not yet shipped. This prototype is a proof of concept for that vision.

System Prompt as PRD

The system prompt written as a product requirements document. Every decision documented with reasoning.

Section 1
Intent Classification with Context

Every message is classified with full conversation history. Short replies like "yes", "ok", or "8.30" are resolved against the previous bot question. A "yes" after a booking offer starts the booking flow. "8.30" after asking for a time is parsed as 8:30 PM. Fuzzy matching handles common spelling errors.
